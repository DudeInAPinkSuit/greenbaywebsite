About Us

At Greenbay Shipping, we specialize in delivering seamless maritime solutions tailored to your cargo handling needs. With years of expertise in midstream loading (Stevedoring/Shipsiding Operations), Ship Agency, Lighterage/Barging Services, Customs Brokerage, Ship Chartering (Foreign/Local), and Heavy Lift Cargo Handling, we're your trusted partner in navigating the seas of logistics.

Mission

Our mission is to be a leading integrated multi-commodity handler, aggressively pursuing cost-efficient door-to-door services to satisfy the fast-growing demand in the Philippines and the Far East.

Services

Bulk Cargo Transport

Efficient and reliable transportation of bulk commodities, including grains, ores, coal, and other raw materials. We specialize in handling large volumes of cargo, ensuring timely delivery and cost-effective solutions.

Customs Brokerage

Navigating complex customs regulations with expertise and efficiency. Our customs brokerage services ensure smooth clearance and compliance, minimizing delays and optimizing the clearance process for your shipments.

Chartering Services
(International & Local)

Facilitating chartering of vessels for both international and local routes. Whether you require a bulk carrier, tanker, or specialized vessel, we arrange charters tailored to your cargo requirements and shipping needs.

Lighterage/Barging Services

Flexible and seamless transfer of cargo between vessels or to ports with limited access. Our lighterage and barging services provide efficient solutions for transporting goods along coastal areas and waterways.
